About

The Entrepreneurial Investor for Growth

WENVEST Capital is a private investment firm, founded by Bernd Wendeln. Having started with traditional Angel Investments, WENVEST Capital emerged to become an entrepreneurial Venture Capital investor for Growth.
The team of WENVEST Capital is dedicated to support the digital leaders of tomorrow with capital, know-how and network.

We are based in Munich and are looking for investment opportunities primarily in Germany but also across other European countries.

Headquartered in Munich, Investing in Germany & Europe

STRATEGY & INVESTMENT CRITERIA

Typical Investment of EUR 0.5m – 1.0m around Series A Phase

We are entrepreneurial minded investors and partners, investing into pre-growth and growth companies, typically around Series A investment phase. With our first investment into a company, we usually invest in the range of EUR 0.5m to EUR 1.0m and reserve further capital for future financing rounds. From taking over board seats, to supporting in future fundraising, strategic & operational sparring or hiring, we can be your partner of choice.
